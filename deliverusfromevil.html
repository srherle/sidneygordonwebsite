Deliver Us From Evil

2022, hand-processed 16mm to digital,
single-channel video with sound, 06:37 min.



**STROBE WARNING**

Images dance in and out of frame and focus while viewers are taken on a psychological journey of an individual trapped inside of a living, breathing building.

Made in reference and response to Hålbå’s emotionally charged Deliver us from Evil; a track composed in three parts, which portrays the story of discovering demons, fighting with them, and living in peace after having defeated them.

Made for SUBVERTED’s Facing Wounds VA release by contacting printing 16mm found footage with a flashlight, sock & my feet, & hand-processing in my small home bathroom.
