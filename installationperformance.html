Central Beam of Light
2022. single channel video.



Audiovisual documentation of collaboration with Sara Gold for the NYE 2022 Bien Agiter stream.

A beam of obscured central light sculpted through variations of optical glass into a pulsing phantom dance.

A 909 sidechained with no input mixers and a vintage analog modular.
